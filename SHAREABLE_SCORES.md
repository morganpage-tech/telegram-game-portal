# Shareable Scores - Quick Guide

## 📤 How to Share Your Scores

### Step 1: Play a Game
- Open any game from the portal
- Play and get a score (any score works!)

### Step 2: Finish the Game
- Click "← Back" to exit the game
- Your score is saved automatically

### Step 3: Click Share Button
- A 📤 share button appears in the header
- Click it to open the share popup

### Step 4: Choose Share Option

#### 📱 Share to Telegram
- Opens Telegram's native share
- Choose a chat or friend
- Sends your score with message

#### 👥 Challenge a Friend
- Opens chat with friend
- Pre-fills challenge message
- Direct link to the game

#### 🔗 Share Link
- Copies score to clipboard
- Share anywhere (WhatsApp, Twitter, etc.)
- Includes game link

### Step 5: Customize Message (Optional)
- Edit the message in the text area
- Use template buttons for quick options:
  - **Default**: Standard message
  - **Funny**: Humorous message
  - **Challenge**: Competitive message

### Step 6: Share!
- Click your chosen share option
- Message is sent or copied
- Done! 🎉

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Share button appears only after finishing a game
- You need a score > 0 to share
- Custom messages are saved per session
- Clipboard works on all platforms
- Telegram sharing works best in Mini App
