# Daily Streaks & Rewards - Quick Guide

## 🎁 How to Claim Daily Rewards

1. **Look for the notification** - A red pulse indicator appears on the 🎁 button in the header
2. **Click the gift icon** - Opens the rewards popup
3. **See your reward** - Check the amount based on your current streak
4. **Click "Claim"** - Receive your coins instantly!
5. **Come back tomorrow** - Rewards reset at midnight

## 🪙 How to Earn Coins

### Daily Rewards
- Day 1: 50 coins
- Day 2: 60 coins
- Day 3: 70 coins
- Day 4: 80 coins
- Day 5: 100 coins
- Day 6: 120 coins
- Day 7+: 150 coins (maximum)

### Playing Games
- **Time bonus**: 1 coin per minute of playtime
- **Score bonus**: 1 coin per 100 points scored
- **Minimum**: Always earn at least 1 coin per session

**Example**:
- Play for 5 minutes + score 500 points = 5 + 5 + 1 = **11 coins**
